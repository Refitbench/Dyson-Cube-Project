# Dyson Cube Program - Backported by Refitbench

## About

This is a [Cleanroom Loader](https://cleanroommc.com/) backport of [Dyson Cube Project](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/dyson-cube-project) by [Buuz135](https://www.curseforge.com/members/buuz135/projects), you can build your very own Dyson Sphere like megastructure in Minecraft and unlock limitless energy for your world.

## Requirements / Compatibility

* This version requires the [Cleanroom Loader](https://cleanroommc.com/). It will not load without it.
* Optional compat with Advanced Rocketry, installing will change the overworld skybox render to include a round dyson sphere.

### 🔧 How it works:

    Send Solar Sails and Beams to the sun using EM Railguns, EM Railguns can only work during non rainy days, and they will stop working when the sun is at its peak (By default you need 1 beam to support 4 solar sails).
    After sending a bunch of solar sails you can harvest the power (FE) using Ray Recievers.

(All multiblocks can be connected from the bottom middle block)

In multiplayer other players can join your sphere by interacting with any multiblock and clicking on the subscribe button.
